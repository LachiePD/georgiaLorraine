    Services offered

    At our home-call doctor service in Burleigh Heads, we are dedicated to providing high-quality medical care to our patients whenever and wherever they need it. Whether you're experiencing a sudden illness, injury, or just need routine care, our team is here to help. We offer a range of services designed to meet your unique needs, from urgent care and home visits to preventative care and chronic disease management. Please take a moment to explore the services we offer and learn more about how we can help you stay healthy and happy.

    Medical assesment/diagnosis

    At our home-call doctor service, Dr. Georgia Lorraine can perform medical assessments and diagnose a wide range of illnesses and injuries. With years of experience in the medical industry, she is skilled at identifying and treating various health concerns.

    Prescriptions

    If you require medication, Dr. Lorraine can prescribe the appropriate treatment and provide advice on usage. She is dedicated to ensuring that patients receive the necessary care and medication they need to recover.

    Wound Care

    Dr. Lorraine can provide expert wound care services, including dressing changes and wound cleaning. Whether you have a minor cut or a more severe wound, she can help ensure proper healing and prevent further complications.

    Intravenous Therapy

    Our home-call doctor service offers intravenous (IV) therapy, including IV fluids and medications. Dr. Lorraine can administer IV therapy when necessary to help alleviate symptoms and promote recovery.

    Pallative Care

    .For patients with serious illnesses, including those nearing the end of their lives, Dr. Lorraine can provide compassionate palliative care services. This includes pain management and symptom relief to improve quality of life.

    Chronic Disease Management

    Dr. Lorraine is experienced in providing ongoing medical care and management for patients with chronic illnesses such as diabetes or heart disease. She works closely with patients to help manage their symptoms and improve their overall health.

    Specialist Referrals

    If further medical evaluation or treatment is needed, Dr. Lorraine can refer patients to specialists. She has a network of trusted specialists who can provide patients with the expert care they need.
